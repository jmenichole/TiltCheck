Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Effective Date: November 2024

1. Service Availability

TiltCheck commits to the following uptime guarantees based on your subscription plan:

Plan Uptime Guarantee Monthly Credit
Starter 99.0% 10% credit if below 99%
Professional 99.5% 15% credit if below 99.5%
Enterprise 99.9% 25% credit if below 99.9%

2. Support Response Times

Plan Response Time Channels
Starter 48 hours Email
Professional 24 hours Email, Phone
Enterprise 4 hours Email, Phone, Dedicated Support

3. Performance Metrics

  • API Response Time: < 500ms average (99th percentile)
  • Alert Delivery: < 2 seconds from detection
  • Data Processing: Real-time processing with < 1 second delay
  • Dashboard Loading: < 3 seconds initial load

4. Maintenance Windows

  • Scheduled Maintenance: Announced 48 hours in advance
  • Maintenance Window: Typically 2-4 hours
  • Frequency: Maximum once per month
  • Timing: Off-peak hours (2 AM - 6 AM EST)

5. Data Backup & Recovery

  • Backup Frequency: Daily automatic backups
  • Retention Period: 30 days
  • Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours
  • Recovery Point Objective (RPO): 24 hours

6. Security Commitments

  • SOC 2 Type II compliance (Enterprise plan)
  • GDPR compliance for all plans
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)

7. Service Credits

If we fail to meet our SLA commitments, you may be eligible for service credits:

  • Credits are applied to your next billing cycle
  • Maximum credit per month: 100% of monthly fee
  • To claim credits, contact support within 30 days of incident

8. Exclusions

This SLA does not cover downtime caused by:

  • Scheduled maintenance (properly announced)
  • Third-party service failures
  • DDoS attacks or other malicious activities
  • Client misuse or violation of terms
  • Force majeure events

9. Monitoring & Reporting

All customers have access to:

  • Real-time status page at tiltcheck.it.com/status
  • Monthly uptime reports
  • Incident post-mortems (Enterprise plan)

10. Contact

For SLA-related questions or to report an incident:

Email: support@tiltcheck.it.com

Enterprise Support: Available 24/7
