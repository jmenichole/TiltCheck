Respect & Inclusivity
Treat all community members with respect, regardless of their gaming experience, background, or beliefs. We celebrate diversity and welcome perspectives from all walks of life.
Responsible Gaming First
Our community prioritizes healthy gaming habits and responsible behavior. Share strategies, support others, and promote awareness about gambling-related risks and interventions.
Constructive Dialogue
Engage in meaningful conversations that add value to the community. Provide helpful feedback, share experiences thoughtfully, and contribute to solutions rather than just highlighting problems.
Transparency & Trust
Be honest about your experiences and affiliations. Support our mission of building trust through transparency in gaming platforms and responsible behavior monitoring.

Stay On Topic
Keep discussions relevant to the category you're posting in. Use appropriate tags to help others find and engage with your content effectively.
No Spam or Self-Promotion
Avoid repetitive posts, excessive self-promotion, or content that doesn't add value to discussions. Share external links only when they're genuinely helpful to the conversation.
Quality Over Quantity
Focus on creating meaningful posts rather than frequent low-quality content. Take time to articulate your thoughts clearly and provide context for your contributions.
Cite Sources & Give Credit
When sharing information, research, or media created by others, always provide proper attribution. This includes crediting creators like blackjackson for audio content and other contributors.

üíö Our Mission

TiltCheck exists to promote responsible gaming through behavioral monitoring, early intervention, and community support. Every discussion should align with these values.

Share Support Resources
When discussing challenges or concerning behaviors, always include links to support resources, helplines, or professional assistance options. Help others find the support they need.
Celebrate Healthy Habits
Highlight positive gaming practices, successful use of intervention tools, and healthy boundary-setting strategies. Share what works and inspire others.
Discuss Behavioral Insights
Share experiences with tilt detection, intervention effectiveness, and behavioral pattern recognition in ways that help the community learn and improve.
