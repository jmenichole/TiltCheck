Frequently Asked Questions
Q: How do I get started with TiltCheck?
A: Check out our Quick Start Guide for installation instructions and basic setup.
Q: What are the different subscription plans?
A: We offer Starter ($299/mo), Professional ($799/mo), and Enterprise (custom pricing) plans. See our homepage for details.
Q: How do I integrate TiltCheck with my Discord server?
A: Check out our Discord Integration Guide in the documentation.
Q: What is the TiltCheck Agent?
A: The TiltCheck Agent is an AI-powered autonomous agent built with Fetch.ai's uAgents framework for real-time tilt detection. See README_AGENT.md for details.
Q: How do I report a bug or request a feature?
A: Please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
Q: What are the API rate limits?
A: Rate limits vary by plan: Starter (100k/mo), Professional (1M/mo), Enterprise (unlimited). See our SLA for details.
